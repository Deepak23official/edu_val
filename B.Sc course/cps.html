B.Sc. Computer Science

Basics of Computer Science

Computer Science focuses on understanding computers, programming, algorithms, and data structures. Students learn programming languages like Python, Java, and C++, as well as concepts such as databases, operating systems, and software development.

  • Data Analyst
  • Web Developer
  • AI/ML Engineer
  • System Administrator

Higher Studies

  • M.Sc. Computer Science
  • MCA (Master of Computer Applications)
  • M.Tech in Data Science or Artificial Intelligence
  • Ph.D. in Computer Science
