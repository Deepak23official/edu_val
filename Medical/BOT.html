BOT (Bachelor of Occupational Therapy)

Basics of BOT

BOT is a professional degree that trains students to assist people with physical, sensory, or cognitive disabilities through therapeutic activities.

Job Opportunities

  • Occupational Therapist
  • Pediatric Therapist
  • Rehabilitation Specialist
  • Clinical Researcher
  • Healthcare Consultant

Higher Studies

  • Master of Occupational Therapy (MOT)
  • Ph.D. in Occupational Therapy
  • Postgraduate Diploma in Rehabilitation Therapy
