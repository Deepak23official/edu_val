BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery)

Basics of BDS

BDS is a professional degree in dentistry that trains students to diagnose and treat dental issues. It includes orthodontics, periodontology, and oral surgery.

Job Opportunities

  • Dentist
  • Orthodontist
  • Periodontist
  • Oral Surgeon
  • Dental Consultant

Higher Studies

  • MDS (Master of Dental Surgery)
  • Ph.D. in Dentistry
  • Fellowship in Dental Specialties
