B.Pharm

Basics of B.Pharm

B.Pharm (Bachelor of Pharmacy) focuses on the study of drugs, their development, and safe usage. It includes pharmacology, pharmaceutical chemistry, and pharmacy practice.

Job Opportunities

  • Pharmacist
  • Drug Inspector
  • Pharmaceutical Researcher
  • Medical Representative
  • Clinical Pharmacist

Higher Studies

  • M.Pharm (Master of Pharmacy)
  • Ph.D. in Pharmacy
  • MBA in Pharmaceutical Management
