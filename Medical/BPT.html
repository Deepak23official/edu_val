BPT (Bachelor of Physiotherapy)

Basics of BPT

BPT is an undergraduate course that prepares students for physiotherapy practices. It involves physical therapy techniques to treat patients with movement disorders and injuries.

Job Opportunities

  • Physiotherapist
  • Sports Therapist
  • Rehabilitation Specialist
  • Clinical Researcher
  • Ergonomic Consultant

Higher Studies

  • MPT (Master of Physiotherapy)
  • Ph.D. in Physiotherapy
  • Postgraduate Diploma in Sports Therapy
