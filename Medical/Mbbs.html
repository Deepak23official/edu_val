MBBS

Basics of MBBS

MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery) is an undergraduate degree that prepares students for a career in medicine. It covers anatomy, pathology, pharmacology, and clinical practice.

Job Opportunities

  • General Physician
  • Surgeon
  • Specialist Doctor
  • Medical Officer
  • Clinical Researcher

Higher Studies

  • MD (Doctor of Medicine)
  • MS (Master of Surgery)
  • DM (Doctorate of Medicine)
  • MCh (Master of Chirurgiae)
